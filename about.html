About de Vries Marine

At de Vries Marine, we specialize in lubrication solutions specifically designed for the harsh conditions of mechanical dredging. Our mission is to deliver cutting-edge automatic lubrication systems and environmentally compliant greases that enhance performance, reduce downtime, and ensure sustainable marine operations. We are committed to engineering excellence and marine environmental responsibility.

With over 25 years of hands-on experience, we have worked extensively with SKF and its Lincoln automatic grease lubrication systems, developing deep expertise in optimizing roller and plain bearing lubrication strategies for demanding dredging environments. This foundation allows us to deliver reliable, field-proven solutions that meet the highest standards of durability and environmental compliance.
