About

At de Vries Marine, we specialize in lubrication solutions specifically designed for the harsh conditions of mechanical dredging. Our mission is to deliver cutting-edge automatic lubrication systems and environmentally compliant greases that enhance performance, reduce downtime, and ensure sustainable marine operations. We are committed to engineering excellence and marine environmental responsibility.

With over 25 years of hands-on experience, we have worked extensively with SKF and its Lincoln automatic grease lubrication systems, developing deep expertise in optimizing roller and plain bearing lubrication strategies for demanding dredging environments. This experience enables us to deliver reliable, field-proven solutions that meet the highest standards of durability and environmental compliance.

Our partnership with one of the premier lubricant developers further allows us to offer tailor-made greases, engineered specifically for the unique mechanical and environmental challenges of dredging applications. This collaborative approach ensures optimal performance and longevity in even the most extreme marine conditions.
